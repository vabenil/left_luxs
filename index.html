Left Lux

Welcome to Left Lux, your go-to platform for saving money and reducing food waste. Our mission is to help you enjoy delicious meals from your favorite local restaurants at a fraction of the original price. Each day, restaurants list their unsold food items at a discounted rate, allowing you to enjoy high-quality meals while helping to minimize waste. Simply search for participating restaurants, place your order, and pick up your meal at your convenience.


How It Works:

  • Search: Find local restaurants with unsold food.
  • Purchase: Buy delicious meals at a discounted price.
  • Pick Up: Collect your meal from the restaurant.

Join our community and enjoy the satisfaction of saving money and helping the environment, one meal at a time.
